      <h1>FootPrintly</h1>
      <p>
        FootPrintly is a mobile application designed to help urban dwellers
        monitor, understand, and improve their sustainability practices. By
        tracking daily behaviors through detailed questionnaires and a dynamic
        scoring system, FootPrintly provides a clear snapshot of your carbon
        footprint while offering actionable insights to enhance your eco-friendly
        lifestyle.
      </p>

      <hr />

          <strong>Meat-Free Monday</strong> to earn rewards and badges.
